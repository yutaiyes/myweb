Design Principles

  • Modern & Clean: Use generous whitespace and clear hierarchy
  • Responsive: Optimize for different screen sizes (750px design width)
  • Accessible: Maintain good contrast ratios and touch targets
  • Delightful: Add subtle animations and transitions
  • Consistent: Follow established patterns throughout the app
